Mixed Tacos

Description

Tacos Mix is a celebration of diverse fillings, bringing together the essence of different culinary traditions.

Ingredients

  • Taco Bread
  • Your Favorite Sauce
  • Chicken And Meat
  • Cheese
  • Potato Fries

Steps

  1. Prepare taco bread
  2. Add flavored sauce
  3. Add cooked chicken and beef
  4. Add Cheese
  5. Add potato fries
  6. Roll it out
  7. Bake it in a pressing machine

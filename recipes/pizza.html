Sea Food Pizza

Description

Seafood pizza is a delicious variation of traditional pizza that incorporates various types of seafood as toppings.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sliced fish
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Prepare the seafood
  2. Chop vegetables and herbs
  3. Assemble the pizza
  4. Add herbs and seasonings
  5. Drizzle with olive oil
  6. Bake in the oven

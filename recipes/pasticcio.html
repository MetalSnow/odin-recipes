Pasticcio

Description

Pasticcio is a savory baked casserole that combines layers of potato fries, a rich meat sauce, and a creamy béchamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Potato Fries
  • Meat Sauce
  • Béchamel Sauce
  • Mozarella
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Prepare potato fries
  2. Add béchamel sauce
  3. Add cooked ground beef
  4. Add mozarella on top
  5. Bake in the oven
